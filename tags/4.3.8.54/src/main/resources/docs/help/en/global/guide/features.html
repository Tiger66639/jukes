Features

If you are looking for light weight , easy to use, powerful and free player and music manager, if you need to organize your music collection and always have your music files at your finger tips, then Jukes is the best solution.

Free

Jukes is distributed under Creative Commons license. You don't need to purchaser new license for each installation and pay for update every year. Just run Jukes at any computer you want.

Cross-platform

You can run Jukes at different computer platforms. It does not matter which platform do you prefer - Windows, Linux or Mac OS, Jukes helps you to reach your music catalog.

Portable

Jukes stores all the data with powerful modern relational database HyperSQL, with dll built into application code. So you never need to install and tune up SQL-server. Just copy Jukes to your flash-drive or another external media and you will always have your music catalog under your hand.

Wide range of music files

Jukes supports all standard audio files such as mp3, mp4, flac, ogg, ape, wma.

Automatically explore data

Select directories with audio files and Jukes will scan all subdirectories, find all audio files and album cover images and write music tag info into database. Jukes will also find all repeating albums.

Easy to set-up and work

Intuitive user interface and flexible parameters system allows user to set-up and tune application very easy. Jukes will have look-and-feel and will operate strictly in accordance with your needs.

Tags

You can add or correct missing or incorrect tag-data, remove, add or copy tag contents between files names and tag fields using batch operations. The powerfull tools allows you to find incomplete or erroneous tags such as wrong Genres.

Search Web

Jukes will help you to find album info and album covers with Amazon and FreeDB internet services.

Managing data

Jukes browser shows all your music catalog in a tree view, together with comprehensive reporting system allowing to find fast and and easy any artist, album or track. Due to flexible filters you can scroll through your music catalog, fill in playlists and lists of albums.

Play

Play any selected track or album with Jukes.
